Heart Collector

Instructions
You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

When you click on a heart, you will add a hidden amount of points to your total score.

If your click total is the same as the random number, you win! If your click total is different from the random number, you lose.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

If your click total is different from the random number, you lose.
